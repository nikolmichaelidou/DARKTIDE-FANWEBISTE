Historic Confrontations in the Warp and Beyond

Throughout the history of the Imperium, psykers have played crucial roles in decisive battles, sometimes determining the fate of entire worlds with their powers.

Legendary Psychic Conflicts

The Battle of Prospero

The tragic confrontation between the Space Wolves and the Thousand Sons, where the psychic might of Magnus the Red and his legion faced the wrath of Leman Russ. The planet became a battleground of devastating psychic energies.

The Emperor vs. Horus

The ultimate psychic duel between the Emperor of Mankind and his fallen son Horus, aboard the Vengeful Spirit. This titanic clash of psychic power decided the fate of humanity itself.

The Burning of Prospero

When the Sisters of Silence and Custodes brought their anti-psychic abilities against the stronghold of the greatest psychic legion of Space Marines, resulting in catastrophic devastation.

The Battle for Cadia

Where Abaddon the Despoiler unleashed hordes of warp-tainted psykers against Imperial defenses, requiring heroic sacrifices from Imperial psykers to counter them.
