The Psyker Classification System

The Imperium classifies psykers on the "Assignment" scale, ranging from Omega (virtually no psychic potential) to Alpha Plus (planet-destroying potential).

Alpha+ Class

Immensely powerful psykers capable of destroying battleships single-handedly or even controlling entire planets. Usually unstable and extremely dangerous. Examples include the Emperor and primarchs.

Alpha Class

Extremely powerful psykers who can affect entire battlefields. Chief Librarians and the most powerful Farseers may reach this level.

Beta Class

Very powerful psykers that include most Space Marine Librarians, Eldar Warlocks, and powerful Astropaths.

Gamma Class

Moderately powerful psykers that include Imperial Sanctioned Psykers and Astropaths.

Delta Class

Mild psykers, often used as Astropaths or for other specialized Imperial services.

Imperial Psyker Types

Space Marine Librarians

The psychic warriors of the Adeptus Astartes. They combine their gene-enhanced physical might with devastating psychic abilities, serving as both warriors and repositories of chapter knowledge.

Astropaths

Blind psykers who specialize in telepathic communication across the vast distances of space. The backbone of Imperial communication.

Sanctioned Imperial Psykers

Psykers who serve with the Imperial Guard, providing psychic support to military operations.

Inquisitorial Psykers

Psykers who serve the Inquisition, using their powers to detect heresy, interrogate suspects, and combat daemonic threats.

Primaris Psykers

Elite combat psykers who serve in the Astra Militarum, capable of unleashing devastating psychic attacks against enemy forces.
